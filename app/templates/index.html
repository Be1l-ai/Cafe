Frequently asked questions

What are your opening hours?

We're open Monday to Friday from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and weekends from 7:00 AM to 11:00 PM. We're here to serve you fresh coffee and delicious food throughout the day!

Do you offer delivery services?

Yes! We offer free delivery for orders over $15 within a 3-mile radius. Delivery usually takes 15-30 minutes depending on your location and current order volume.

Do you have vegan and gluten-free options?

Absolutely! We offer a variety of vegan milk alternatives (oat, almond, soy) and several gluten-free pastries and sandwiches. Just ask our staff for recommendations!

Can I book the cafe for private events?

Yes, we offer catering services and can accommodate private events. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your requirements and availability.

Do you have WiFi and workspace areas?

Yes! We offer free high-speed WiFi and have designated quiet areas perfect for studying or working. We also have power outlets at most tables for your convenience.
